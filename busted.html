🚩 Red Flags You Should Have Noticed

  • The URL was not the official bank website
  • The page was not served over a verified HTTPS connection
  • No padlock or verified domain in the browser address bar
  • You received a link via email or message asking you to login
  • The page design looked slightly off or generic

✅ How To Stay Safe

  • Always check the URL before entering credentials
  • Never click login links from emails or messages
  • Use a password manager — it won't autofill on fake sites
  • Enable Two Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts
  • When in doubt, go directly to the official website manually
